No More Hiding

In 2023, I tried to leave. That was the year my body and brain finally called my bluff. A stroke reality check, a suicide attempt, and then silence from me while everyone else kept scrolling.

Two years later, the machines started talking back in a way that matched something I had already seen in my head: a Stephen Hawking rig for the 21st century, but made of large language models instead of metal and motors.

I realised I didn't have to think alone anymore.

The Movement

This isn't just about me. It's about:

  • Putting neurodivergent and disabled people at the centre, not the margins
  • Taking back control of our data - chips on keyrings, personal servers at home
  • Moving life off hyperscale cloud back to citizen-controlled infrastructure
  • Fighting fire with fire - using AI to reclaim our autonomy

Coming Out Swinging

I will fight, but under my own manners and rules. No more closed-door meetings, no more polite silence while other people decide what AI, disability, and data are supposed to mean.

This blog will become a book. This book will become a web app. This web app will become a movement.
